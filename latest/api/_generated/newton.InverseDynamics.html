newton.InverseDynamics#

class newton.InverseDynamics(articulation_count, joint_dof_count, max_dofs_per_articulation, body_count, max_joints_per_articulation, world_count, device=None)[source]#

Bases: object

Inverse dynamics quantities for a batch of articulated rigid-body systems.

class EvalType(*values)#

Bases: IntFlag

Bitmask flags selecting which quantities InverseDynamics should compute.

Flags can be combined with bitwise-or to request multiple quantities simultaneously; ALL is the union of all individual flags.

ALL = 7#

Compute the mass matrix, gravity force, and Coriolis force.

CORIOLIS_FORCE = 4#

Compute the Coriolis force C(q, q_dot)*q_dot.

GRAVITY_FORCE = 2#

Compute the gravity force g(q) = ∂U/∂q.

MASS_MATRIX = 1#

Compute the joint-space mass matrix M(q).

__init__(articulation_count, joint_dof_count, max_dofs_per_articulation, body_count, max_joints_per_articulation, world_count, device=None)#

Allocate output buffers for inverse dynamics.

The mass matrix is stored in the shape expected by eval_mass_matrix(): (articulation_count, max_dofs_per_articulation, max_dofs_per_articulation). max_dofs_per_articulation already includes any 6 DOFs contributed by a floating-base root joint.

The gravity_force and coriolis_force buffers share the flat joint-space layout that joint_qd uses.

Internal RNEA/Jacobian scratch is allocated once and held privately on this instance; callers do not manage it. Prefer Model.inverse_dynamics() over calling this constructor directly.

Parameters:

  • articulation_count (int) – Number of articulations (matches Model.articulation_count).

  • joint_dof_count (int) – Total number of joint DOFs across all articulations (matches Model.joint_dof_count).

  • max_dofs_per_articulation (int) – Per-articulation DOF count (inclusive of floating-base root DOFs, if any). Matches Model.max_dofs_per_articulation.

  • body_count (int) – Total number of rigid bodies (matches Model.body_count); sizes the internal RNEA scratch.

  • max_joints_per_articulation (int) – Maximum joints in any articulation (matches Model.max_joints_per_articulation); sizes the internal Jacobian scratch.

  • world_count (int) – Number of simulation worlds (matches Model.world_count); sizes the internal zero-gravity input.

  • device (Device | str | None) – Warp device on which the buffers are allocated.

coriolis_force: wp.array(dtype=wp.float32, ndim=1)#

Coriolis + centrifugal force C(q, q_dot)*q_dot [N or N·m, depending on joint type], shape (joint_dof_count,), dtype float. The joint-space force a controller must apply to maintain the current joint_qd at zero acceleration in the absence of gravity.

gravity_force: wp.array(dtype=wp.float32, ndim=1)#

Gravity force g(q) = ∂U/∂q [N or N·m, depending on joint type], shape (joint_dof_count,), dtype float. The joint-space force a controller must apply to hold the articulation static under gravity.

mass_matrix: wp.array(dtype=wp.float32, ndim=3)#

Joint-space mass matrix M(q) [kg, kg·m, or kg·m^2, depending on the joint types of the row/column DOFs], shape (articulation_count, max_dofs_per_articulation, max_dofs_per_articulation), dtype float.

tau: wp.array(dtype=wp.float32, ndim=1)#

Inverse-dynamics joint force tau = M(q)*qddot + C(q, q_dot)*q_dot + g(q) [N or N·m, depending on joint type], shape (joint_dof_count,), dtype float. Populated by eval_inverse_dynamics_force() from the other buffers in this container plus a user-supplied qddot. eval_inverse_dynamics() does not write this buffer; it remains zero until eval_inverse_dynamics_force() is called.
